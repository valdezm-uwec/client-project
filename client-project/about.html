About

Why Fitness?

Living your best life and being content where you are in this wonderful thing called LIFE. We are not promised a life without trouble, but we are living so let's make the best of it. I'm passionate about helping people feel comfortable in their own skin through the changes that life brings their way. Our team is here for you in the ups and downs of life.

Fitness Journey

A tired, frazzled mom. A burned out teacher skipping breakfast. That's where I was when I found BODi. After getting off of a Disney cruise, I felt fluffy from a lack of daily fitness and a bit overwhelmed with my nutrition over the previous 10 days of the all you can eat buffets and dessert bars. I wanted to change. I needed to change. I also had this nagging feeling that God had something else in store for me. I wanted friendship, fitness, and fun all wrapped up in a neat package. What's interesting is I didn't know I wanted it until after I said YES to my coach, Deb. Her guidance (and patience) led me to this team after being an active Beachbody customer for 10 months. Coaching was a part of this business that I never thought I wanted or needed. It has changed my life. More friends. More fitness. More fun. More YES moments in my life.

Personal Life

My mission to share God's love with students, especially 5th graders, has continued for 27 years. I'm blessed to teach at a school that puts Christ first. When I'm not teaching I enjoy working at a local winery where I get to meet people from all over the country and share tropical fruit wines with them. In summer you can find me at the beach, on a golf course, or at a country concert.
