Favorites

Favorites

Having favorite things that you come back to time and time again helps create positive routines. If you LOVE something, you are sure to use it. If you never try new things, you'll never know if you will love them or not.

Tips

My favorite tip for sticking with anything like fitness, nutrition, lesson planning, painting a room, going on a trip is to plan for it! If you fail to plan, you plan to fail.

Morning Routine

  1. Wake up
  2. Get workout clothes on
  3. Drink Energize (get up and GO juice)
  4. Go on zoom to see online fitness friends, press play on workout

Evening Routine

  1. Read the Bible
  2. Get ready for bed
  3. Set my alarm
  4. Shoot for 7-8 hours of sleep
  5. Know what program or workout you will do tomorrow

Recipies

I used to skip breakfast. As a teacher and busy mom, I'd opt for sleep over sustenance. If I really planned ahead, I'd grab a bagel and smear cream cheese on it, shove it in a napkin, and eat it on the way to school. Now I drink Shakeology on my way to school every single day to keep me fueled. This superfood nutrition shake curbs my sweet cravings, is an easy-grab and go, helps with weight loss, and tastes delicious. No brainer!

Shakeology Recipe

  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 handful fresh spinach
  • ½ scoop of powder peanut butter
  • 2 T hemp seeds
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 1 scoop of chocolate Shakeology
