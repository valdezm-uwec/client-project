What is ZYIA Active?

Erin Bradley, CEO and Owner of ZYIA Active states, “We talked about my inspiration during that moonlit hike and turned my impressions into the four pillars at ZYIA's foundation: Light, Activity, Community, and Uplift. Even the beauty of that moon, the symbol that sparked it all, now serves as our logo. ZYIA offers many ways to enhance your life. If nothing else, I hope it adds light to your journey.

Outside of teaching and partnering with BODi since April of 2018, I enjoy being a ZYIA rep where I can share affordable, quality fitness and athleisure clothing with women and those they love.

Whether you are new to ZYIA Active or looking to expand your collection, there is something for everyone! Let me know if there is anything specific you would like me to help you style. Our line is high quality, versatile, affordable, and stylish. I can't wait for you to fall in love with your new threads!
